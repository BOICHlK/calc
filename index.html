How to Use

Roll Length: Enter the total length of the roll.

Setup: Specify the setup length required for the machine.

Buffer: Enter an additional margin (from both sides) around the defect.

Defect Start & End: Enter defect positions (from the first pass).

Defect Name: Enter the type or short description.

Add Row: Adds a new defect line.

Remove (X): Removes the corresponding defect row.

Calculate: Merges close defects (< 200 m) and processes in reverse order.
